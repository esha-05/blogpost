Ankur Warikoo

Ankur Warikoo is an Indian entrepreneur, investor, and motivational speaker. He was born on May 25, 1980, in Delhi, India. Warikoo completed his schooling from St. Columba's School in Delhi and went on to earn a Bachelor's degree in Economics from Hindu College, University of Delhi. He later pursued an MBA from the Indian School of Business (ISB) in Hyderabad.
